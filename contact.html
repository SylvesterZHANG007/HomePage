Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common academic inquiries

What research areas are you most interested in collaborating on?

I'm particularly interested in algebraic combinatorics projects involving cluster algebras, Schubert calculus, symmetric functions, and representation theory. I also welcome interdisciplinary connections with other areas of mathematics.

Are you available for research mentoring?

Yes, I actively mentor students through programs like PACE (PKU Algebraic Combinatorics Experience) and the UMN REU program. I'm always interested in working with motivated students on research projects.

Do you give talks or presentations?

I regularly present at mathematics conferences and seminars. I'm available for invited talks on algebraic combinatorics topics, particularly those related to my research in cluster algebras and related areas.

How can prospective graduate students connect with you?

Prospective students interested in algebraic combinatorics are welcome to reach out. I can provide information about the graduate program at University of Minnesota and research opportunities in our group.
